Data sources - 

________________________________________________________________
Shipment values:

All ASM data retreived from the U.S. Census Bureau via their API:
https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/asm/data/data-tools.html

For example, to retreive NAICS: 3256207, Years 2002-2016:
https://api.census.gov/data/timeseries/asm/product?get=GEO_TTL,PRODVAL,PSCODE_TTL&for=us:*&time=from+2000+to+2016&PSCODE=3256207

ASM shipment value spreadsheets can also be downloaded from:
https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/asm/data/tables.html
________________________________________________________________
Commodity prices:

For Paint & Coatings:
U.S. Census Bureau, Paint and Allied Products - 2010, MA325F(10), Issued July 2011
https://www.census.gov/data/tables/time-series/econ/cir/ma325f.html
ma325f10.xls

For all others:
Bureau of Transportation Statistics and U.S. Census Bureau, 2012 Commodity Flow Survey, EC12TCF-US, Issued February 2015
ec12tcf-us.pdf
________________________________________________________________
Producer price indicies:

All producer price indicies retreived from FRED:
https://fred.stlouisfed.org/categories/31
